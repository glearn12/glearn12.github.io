my experience as a marketing intern at beats by dre

Internship Overview
I worked on the Music & Culture Brand Marketing Team to create strategic briefs for global product campaigns for Beats Solo Pro and Studio 3. This included selling in music and sports talent and collaborating with Apple Music and external partners to create campaign assets including: Beats by Dre playlists, 2D images, and video assets.

Timeline: June - September 2019

Skills: Positioning Strategy - User POV - Product Marketing

solo pro campaign /1

The majority of my summer was spent developing marketing positioning strategy for the Beats Solo Pro, released in October 2019.

While I can’t discuss the specific strategy of the campaign, my team developed a Gen-Z-targeted campaign using prominent music talents, Billie Eilish and Pharrell Williams, to showcase the product features and design. My role was to write positioning strategy for campaign assets to brief internal and external creative partners.

One of my specific responsibilities was briefing talent on playlist collaborations. This included writing sample Apple Music playlist copy and working with our internal digital team to create playlist assets and get talent approval.

studio 3 camo collection /2

My secondary project was working on the Studio 3 Camo Collection campaign.

While I can’t discuss the specific strategy of the campaign, we developed a Millennial, male-targeted campaign using music and sports talent (James Harden and A$AP Ferg) to showcase the product features and design.

My role was assisting my team in pitching talent and working alongside them to manage campaign 2D, video, and playlist assets.
